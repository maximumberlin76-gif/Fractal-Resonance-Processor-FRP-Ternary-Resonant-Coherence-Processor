The processor therefore continuously attempts to regulate distributed synchronization tension.

## 6. Adaptive Tension Regulation

Adaptive regulation attempts to maintain dynamically sustainable coherent phase interaction conditions between competing synchronization structures.

The framework assumes that stable coherent retention requires balanced phase-tension distribution across distributed resonance topology.
